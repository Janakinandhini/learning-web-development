Unlearning
Unlearning is the new learning

The process of learning has significantly evloved in the past decade. The art of unlearning is essential in today's education and work culture.

Unlearning is the process of discarding something from one's memory. The more we can unlearn,
the more likely we can encounter the growth and progress we desire. There are seven tips for the unlearning process.

  • Having the sense of curiosity
  • Having pit-stops and Continuous revisiting
  • Nurturing the art of willingness
  • Learn from the unlike minds
  • Pursuing the new
  • knowing the goals and clear perceptions
  • Changing the whereabouts
